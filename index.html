Studio Ghibli is an animated studio based in Japan that was founded June 15th, 1985 by Hayao Miyazaki, Toshio Suzuki, and Isao Takahata. The Studio's intention was to "blow a new wind through the anime industry", but its impact soon blew past its borders.
